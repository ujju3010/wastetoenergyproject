About Us

We are a dynamic hackathon team passionate about creating sustainable solutions for urban waste management. Our project focuses on developing innovative waste-to-energy plants across India to tackle the growing problem of waste disposal while generating renewable energy.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize waste management through innovative and sustainable practices that benefit both the environment and communities. By harnessing the potential of waste-to-energy technology, we aim to reduce landfill use, lower carbon emissions, and promote a circular economy.
