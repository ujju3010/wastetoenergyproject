About Us

We are on a mission to design and implement decentralized micro-waste-to-energy systems in urban areas. Our systems efficiently convert organic waste from communities into renewable energy and valuable by-products, ensuring minimal environmental impact. By tackling waste at the neighborhood level, we aim to create sustainable and energy-independent urban communities.
